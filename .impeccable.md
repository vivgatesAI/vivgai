## Design Context

### Users
AI leaders (executives, product leaders, technical decision-makers) who need to quickly evaluate the significance of AI news for their organizations. They scan dozens of articles and need rapid signal-from-noise analysis.

### Brand Personality
Sharp, authoritative, editorial — like a premium intelligence briefing. Not playful, not sterile. Three words: **informed, decisive, clear**.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Dark editorial theme inspired by premium news analysis sites
- Instrument Serif for display/headings (distinctive, editorial feel)
- Geist for body/UI text (clean, technical, modern)
- Teal/emerald accent (oklch 0.72 0.16 170) — not cyan, not purple
- Asymmetric layouts, generous whitespace, 72ch max-width for reading
- No gradients, no glassmorphism, no neon, no side-stripe borders

### Design Principles
1. Signal over noise — every pixel should help the user make a faster decision
2. Editorial authority — the UI should feel like a premium briefing, not a dashboard
3. Progressive disclosure — show what matters first, reveal depth on demand ( Analyse button )
4. Respect the reading experience — cap line lengths, high contrast, clear hierarchy
5. Distinctive, not generic — avoid the AI slop palette (cyan-on-dark, purple gradients)
